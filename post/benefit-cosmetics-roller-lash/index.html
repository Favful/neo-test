Benefit Cosmetics Roller Lash

Enjoy wearing curled lashes for up to 12 hours. This mascara creates the wide-awake effect while strengthening your lashes. Its innovative brush is coated in tiny, soft hooks that grab your lashes gently. It also prevents from your lashes smudging or flaking.

Key benefits and ingredients:

  • Long wearing, black ink formula
  • Contains provitamin B5 and Serin for lash conditioning benefits
  • Easy to remove

How To Use:

  • Open mascara tube and wipe excess mascara off the wand on the inside rim of mascara tube, or on a tissue.
  • Lightly coat curled lashes, starting from lash line then wiggle wand back and forth towards the tip.
