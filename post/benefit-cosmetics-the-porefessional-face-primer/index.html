Benefit Cosmetics The Porefessional Face Primer

The Porefessionla Face Primer is a professional balm that minimizes the appearance of pores.

Key benefits and ingredients:

Quickly minimize the appearance of pores and fine lines and help makeup stay put with this priming phenomenon. The silky and lightweight formula can be worn under makeup to create a smoother-than-smooth canvas or used throughout the day to mattify shine and touch up problem areas. It’s so translucent, no one will know you’re wearing anything!

As a primer, apply a fine layer on clean or moisturized skin, starting from the center of your face and working outwards. Pat to blend. Feel free to use for midday touch-ups too  just dab a pea-size amount over your makeup and blend.
