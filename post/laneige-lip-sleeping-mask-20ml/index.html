Laneige Lip Sleeping Mask

Exfoliating Balm restores smoothness and softness of the skin of the lips.

Key benefits and ingredients:

  • Intensively moisturises and nourishes the delicate skin of the lips, prevents peeling and cracking of the skin.
  • Berries extracts slow down the skin ageing process, neutralise harmful radicals in the body. Give skin a natural, gentle glow, strengthen blood vessels, moisturise the skin, so that it becomes fresh and smooth.

How To Use:

Apply a small amount onto the skin of the lips. Gently massage and wipe the exfoliated skin with a tissue. Leave overnight.

Please keep away from direct sunlight.
