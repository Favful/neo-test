Benefit Cosmetics They’re Real! Lengthening & Volumizing Mascara

This product is a lengthening mascara that curls, volumizes, lifts, and separates lashes for an "out-of-here" look—now available in three vibrant shades.

Key benefits and ingredients:

This breakthrough bestseller reveals lashes you never knew you had! The specially designed brush features staggered bristles that grab close to the root, boosting length and volume beyond belief. The custom-domed tip uses precision bristles to lift, define, and curl even your tiniest lashes. With a glossy, jet-black, long-wearing formula, you’ll flaunt luxurious, silky lashes that won’t smudge, clump, or dry out.

Suggested Usage:

-Position wand horizontally and wiggle from side to side and base to tip to achieve maximum volume and length and lift.

-Hold wand vertically and stroke upward to curl and separate.

-Layer generously as desired.
